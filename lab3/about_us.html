Welcome to the E-library!

We believe that books have the power to change the world. They are able to teach, inspire, give new knowledge and emotions. We want everyone to have the opportunity to immerse themselves in the world of literature, regardless of location, financial situation or other circumstances.

Our mission:

  • Provide free access to a wide range of books in digital format.
  • Make the reading process convenient and pleasant for all users.
  • To preserve and popularize Ukrainian and world literature.

How you can help:

You can help us develop the online library,

  • By spreading information about the site among your friends and acquaintances.
  • Leaving feedback and suggestions about the website.
  • Supporting us financially.
